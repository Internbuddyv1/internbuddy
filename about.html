About InternBuddy

InternBuddy is your automated career assistant, built to streamline applications, track opportunities and help you land your next internship or role with less chaos and more intention.

Why InternBuddy Exists

Applying for internships and entry-level roles can easily turn into a full-time job: dozens of tabs open, spreadsheets everywhere, and the constant feeling you’re forgetting something important. InternBuddy was created to remove that noise.

What began as a personal project to stay on top of applications evolved into a product focused on structure, automation and clarity — so you can spend less time copying details into forms and more time actually preparing for interviews.

Our Mission

We want every student and recent graduate to have a fair shot at opportunities — regardless of background, local networks or how “polished” their CV is at the start.

InternBuddy’s mission is to give you the tools and workflows to run your job search like a well-organised project: clear pipeline, smart tracking, and practical support along the way.

Our Story

The first version of InternBuddy lived entirely in Klevis’s browser: a mix of scripts, small tools and templates that quietly kept track of roles and deadlines. It worked — and it led to multiple offers.

Seeing the impact, the project grew into a dedicated platform. Today, InternBuddy is shaped with feedback from students, grads and early-career professionals who want one place to manage searching, applying and following up.

Our Team

Behind InternBuddy is a small group of engineers, designers and career enthusiasts who care about building tools that feel genuinely helpful, not overwhelming. We prioritise clarity, focus and a product that fits around your life — not the other way around.
