About InternBuddy

InternBuddy is your automated career assistant, designed to help you streamline your job application process and land your dream internship or job.

About the Founder

Hi, I'm Klevis Zaimi, the founder of InternBuddy. I created this platform because I understand the challenges of finding the right internship or job in today's competitive market. Initially, InternBuddy was designed as a personal tool to help me land my dream job. However, I soon realized its potential to assist students worldwide in their career pursuits. Born in Albania on July 7, 2005, I pursued Computer Science at London South Bank University. Since then, I've successfully secured multiple job offers using InternBuddy, proving its effectiveness. I'm passionate about leveraging technology to empower students and make the job search process more accessible and efficient for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students and recent graduates by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed in their job search. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to pursue their career goals without the stress and overwhelm of traditional job applications.

Our Story

InternBuddy was founded by a group of students who experienced firsthand the challenges of the job application process. Frustrated by the time and effort required to apply to multiple jobs, they set out to create a solution that would automate the process and make it easier for students to find internships and jobs.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate engineers, designers, and career experts dedicated to building the best possible career automation platform. We are committed to providing our users with the highest quality service and support.
